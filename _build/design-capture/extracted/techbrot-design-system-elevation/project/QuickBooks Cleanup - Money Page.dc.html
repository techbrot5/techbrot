Questions, answered plainly

Will you work inside my existing QuickBooks file?
Yes. We work in your existing QuickBooks Online or Desktop file — we don't make you switch tools to work with us. If a migration genuinely serves you, we'll scope that separately and explain why.
How long does a QuickBooks cleanup take?
It depends on how many months you're behind and your transaction volume. We scope the work in writing within 3 business days of the discovery call, and most cleanups are delivered in weeks, not months.
What will a cleanup cost?
A fixed monthly fee, set in writing before any work begins. Engagements fall into Essentials ($400–$700/mo), Standard ($700–$1,400/mo), and Complex ($1,400–$2,500+/mo) tiers — your exact fee is scoped from your file, never billed by the surprise hour.
Are you affiliated with Intuit?
No. We are an independent firm of Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisors — not Intuit, and not affiliated with Intuit. For Intuit billing, login, or account questions, contact Intuit directly. For the work inside your file — cleanup, reconciliation, and advisory — that's us.
What happens after the cleanup?
You get reconciled, CPA-ready books and a short note on what changed. From there you can take it back in-house, hand it to your CPA, or keep us on for ongoing bookkeeping and advisory — your choice, scoped in writing.
