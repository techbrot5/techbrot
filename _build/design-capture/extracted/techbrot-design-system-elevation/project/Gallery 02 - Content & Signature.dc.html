Top-of-page. The five intent questions for this page, answered crisply. Marked speakable.

What is a QuickBooks cleanup?
Bringing a behind or broken file current: every transaction categorised, every account reconciled, books made CPA-ready.
How long does it take?
Scoped in writing within 3 business days; most are delivered in weeks, not months.
What does it cost?
A fixed monthly fee, set in writing first — Essentials $400–$700, Standard $700–$1,400, Complex $1,400–$2,500+.

Will you work in my existing file?
Yes — your existing QuickBooks Online or Desktop file. We don't make you switch tools.
Are you affiliated with Intuit?
No. We're an independent firm of Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisors. For Intuit billing or login issues, contact Intuit directly; for the work in your file, that's us.
What happens after the cleanup?
Reconciled, CPA-ready books and a short note on what changed — then it's your choice to continue.
