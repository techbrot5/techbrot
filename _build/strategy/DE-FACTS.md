## VERIFIED DELAWARE TAX FACTS (with sources)
- **No sales tax** — Delaware imposes NO state or local sales tax (0% everywhere; no county/city/special rates).
  Source: DE Division of Revenue. A bookkeeper must NOT set up sales-tax collection for DE.
- **Gross Receipts Tax (GRT)** — Delaware's substitute for a sales tax. Levied on the **SELLER's total gross
  receipts** (not collected from customers). Rates **0.0945%–1.9914%** by business activity (petroleum up to
  2.4218%). A **monthly/quarterly exclusion amount** reduces the taxable base before tax. Filed **monthly or
  quarterly** with the Division of Revenue. QuickBooks must track receipts **by business-activity category**.
  Source: revenue.delaware.gov (Gross Receipts Tax FAQs; rate schedule).
- **Corporate income tax 8.7%** — flat, on federal taxable income apportioned to DE (three-factor). DE businesses
  pay GRT **and** corporate income tax. Source: revenue.delaware.gov.
- **Personal income tax 2.2%–6.6%** (graduated; top rate 6.6%). Source: Tax Foundation / DE Div. of Revenue.
- **Franchise tax** (annual, every DE entity, for the privilege of being a DE entity):
  - **LLC / LP / GP: flat $300/yr, due June 1.** No annual report required for these.
  - **Corporations: $175 minimum** (Authorized Shares method) or **$400 minimum** (Assumed Par Value method),
    **max $200,000** ($250,000 for a Large Corporate Filer), **due March 1**, with an **annual report**.
    Two methods allowed; the entity picks the lower. Source: corp.delaware.gov (franchise tax instructions).
  - Late penalty: $200 + 1.5%/mo interest. **TechBrot does NOT file the franchise tax/annual report** — the
    registered agent or CPA files; TechBrot tracks & reserves for it in the books and confirms the lower method.
- **Wilmington city tax** — **1.25% earned-income (wage) tax** on residents AND non-residents who work in the
  city; plus **1.25% net-profits tax** on sole-proprietor/partnership net profit within city limits. Source:
  wilmingtonde.gov. Only Wilmington has this; Dover/Newark/Middletown/Smyrna/Bear do NOT.
- **Incorporation capital** — **66.7% of the Fortune 500** incorporated in DE; **2.1 million+** active legal
  entities; **81.4% of U.S. IPOs** choose DE; the **DGCL** + **Court of Chancery** are why. Source: corp.delaware.gov.
  HONEST FRAMING: most of those entities are formed by out-of-state owners and never operate in DE. TechBrot
  serves OPERATING DE businesses AND out-of-state owners who need real bookkeeping for their DE entity.
