Is QuickBooks Desktop Enterprise the right fit for my business?

QuickBooks Desktop Enterprise is built for businesses with complex accounting needs and scales as your business grows. Consider it if you:

(i) Manage advanced pricing or have multiple pricing rules,

(ii) Have employees in specialized roles or functions,

(iii) Maintain significant inventory,

(iv) Operate across multiple locations.

For smaller businesses with simpler accounting, QuickBooks Online may be a more suitable choice, offering anywhere, anytime access.

Which QuickBooks Desktop products are available?

QuickBooks Desktop offers a complete suite of business solutions, including:

(i) Accounting and reporting,

(ii) Inventory management,

(iii) Pricing and job costing,

(iv) Payroll and time tracking.

These tools are customizable and work together as an all-in-one platform that grows with your business.

How do I upgrade from QuickBooks Desktop Pro or Premier to Desktop Enterprise?

To upgrade to Desktop Enterprise:

(i) Install Desktop Enterprise software,

(ii) Back up and migrate your data from Pro or Premier,

(iii) Set up permissions and activate your Enterprise subscription.

For medium-sized businesses, it’s recommended to perform the upgrade after hours or on weekends to avoid workflow disruption. Need help? Call us at (833) 225-4401.

Is QuickBooks Desktop Enterprise easy to learn?

QuickBooks Desktop Enterprise is intuitive and user-friendly, recognized as a top mid-market accounting solution. Key points include:

(i) If you already use Desktop Pro or Premier, the interface feels familiar,

(ii) The intuitive design allows fast workflows,

(iii) Minimal learning curve ensures your team can start immediately.

How secure is my data with Desktop Enterprise?

Your data is protected with top-level security features, including:

(i) AES 256-bit encryption,

(ii) Seamless background updates,

(iii) Regular enhancements to keep your accounting software secure and up-to-date.
