    Data security

    Your data stays yours. Always.

    The single biggest data-security risk in outsourced bookkeeping is the parallel-system model — where a provider exports your financials into their own platform, leaving you without control of your own books. TechBrot doesn’t work that way.

    We operate as an accountant-user inside your existing QuickBooks file. You grant access; you can revoke it at any time. Your books never leave your control. The QuickBooks audit trail records every action we take and remains visible to you continuously — you can see exactly what was changed, when, and by whom.

    All working sessions use encrypted screen-share connections; file transfers, when needed, use encrypted channels. We follow documented data-handling practices appropriate for sensitive financial information, detailed in our data processing terms and privacy policy.

    The principle is simple: you hold the keys. We’re a credentialed professional working in your house, not a vendor who takes your house away to work on it.

    Honest scope

    What we do, don't do, and coordinate.

    Trust includes being honest about limits. Here’s the clean read — stated up front, not buried.

    • We do

      Bookkeeping (monthly, cleanup, catch-up). QuickBooks setup, cleanup, migration. Payroll management. Multi-state sales tax compliance. Fractional CFO. CPA-ready year-end handoff. State and local jurisdiction tax operational configuration.

    • We don't

      File federal or state tax returns. Represent clients before the IRS or state agencies. Provide Intuit’s software-level technical support. Publish unverified testimonials or inflated performance claims.

    • We coordinate with

      Your existing CPA or EA for tax filings. Your tax counsel for audit defense. Sales tax counsel for state-specific tax-position work. We refer to licensed practitioners when scope requires it.
