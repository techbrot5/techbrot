TechBrot action button — ink primary (never cobalt), hairline secondary, quiet ghost. Labels are sentence case and verb-first.

```jsx
<Button variant="primary" size="lg">Book a systems review</Button>
<Button variant="secondary">Read the framework</Button>
<Button variant="ghost" size="sm">Cancel</Button>
```

- `variant`: `primary` (ink) | `secondary` (white + hairline) | `ghost`
- `size`: `sm` 32px | `md` 40px | `lg` 48px
- `href` turns it into a link; `disabled` dims to 45%.
- Never: gradients, cobalt fills, pill radius, "Get started!" copy.
