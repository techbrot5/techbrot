Content card — white, hairline border, flat by default. With `href`, the whole card links and raises on hover.

```jsx
<Card eyebrow="Research" title="What a clean month-end looks like" href="/research/month-end">
  <p>We audited 247 QuickBooks files to find why closing takes 25 days for some teams and 3 for others.</p>
</Card>
```

No colored left borders, no icons-for-decoration, no shadows at rest.
