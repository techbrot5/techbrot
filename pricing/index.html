    How does TechBrot price services?

    Why no hourly billing?

    Hourly billing penalizes clients for a provider’s inefficiency and creates unpredictable invoices. TechBrot fixes the fee against a written scope so total cost is known before work starts. Scope changes are re-quoted and re-approved in writing — never a surprise invoice.

    What sets the price within a range?

    Recurring: transaction volume, number of accounts, payroll complexity, entities. Cleanup/catch-up: months or years involved, severity of issues. QB projects: file complexity, integrations, platform. The discovery call surfaces these; the written scope reflects them.

    Is the discovery call free? Any contracts?

    Free, 30 minutes, no obligation. Written fixed-fee scope within 3 business days. No long-term lock-in on recurring engagements — they run month to month against the written scope.

    Does the price include tax filing?

    No. TechBrot delivers operational accounting and CPA-ready books; your CPA or EA files taxes. Year-end CPA handoff is included free in recurring engagements. TechBrot does not file returns or represent clients before the IRS.

    Three engagement shapes

    Every engagement fits one of three shapes.

    Pricing structure follows the shape of the work. All three run to the same standard: fixed fee, written scope, named ProAdvisor.

    • Recurring

      Monthly retainer

      Ongoing work billed as a fixed monthly fee. Same-shape deliverables every month. No long-term lock-in — runs month to month against the written scope.

      For: monthly bookkeeping, payroll, sales tax compliance, fractional CFO.

    • One-time

      Fixed-fee project

      Defined-scope work delivered in weeks, with a written fixed fee and set start and completion dates. Often transitions into a recurring engagement after.

      For: cleanup, catch-up, QuickBooks setup, migration, emergency fixes.

    • By application

      Senior retainer

      Senior strategic engagements accepted by application with limited capacity per quarter. Scoped to the depth of involvement the business needs.

      For: fractional CFO, complex multi-entity advisory.

    Why fixed-fee

    Why we don't bill by the hour.

    Hourly billing has a structural problem: it penalizes you for the provider’s inefficiency. The slower they work, the more you pay. The invoice is unpredictable, and you can’t budget around it. Worse, it makes you hesitate to call your own accountant — because every question starts a meter.

    TechBrot prices every engagement as a fixed fee against a written scope. You know the total cost before any work begins. Call as often as you need within the engagement — no meter. If the work genuinely expands beyond the agreed scope, the fee is re-quoted and re-approved in writing before anything proceeds. You are never surprised by an invoice.

    This only works because the work is delivered by Certified ProAdvisors efficient enough to price confidently. The fixed fee is a statement of competence: we know how long good work takes, so we can quote it.
