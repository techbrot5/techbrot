    Why fixed-fee

    Why we don't bill by the hour.

    Hourly billing has a structural problem: it penalizes you for the provider's inefficiency. The slower they work, the more you pay. The invoice is unpredictable, and you can't budget around it. Worse, it makes you hesitate to call your own accountant — because every question starts a meter.

    TechBrot prices every New York engagement as a fixed fee against a written scope. You know the total cost before any work begins. Call as often as you need within the engagement — no meter. If the work genuinely expands beyond the agreed scope, the fee is re-quoted and re-approved in writing before anything proceeds. You are never surprised by an invoice.

    This works because the work is delivered by Certified ProAdvisors efficient enough to price confidently — including the New York specifics, from NYC sales tax to UBT-ready books. The fixed fee is a statement of competence: we know how long good work takes, so we can quote it.
