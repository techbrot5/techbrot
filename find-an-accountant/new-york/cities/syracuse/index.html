    The Syracuse tax fact we build into your books

    Syracuse sales tax, at a glance.

    8%

    Combined sales tax in Syracuse — 4% New York State plus 4% Onondaga County. No separate city sales tax. Tracked and reconciled in QuickBooks for an accurate quarterly NYS return.

    1

    Web File return — New York centralizes filing, so however many jurisdictions you sell into, you file one NYS sales-tax return. Destination-based: you charge the rate where the customer takes delivery.

    $110

    Clothing & footwear under $110 per item are exempt from the 4% state rate statewide; Onondaga County's local share may still apply. We flag taxable-vs-exempt so the books are right.
