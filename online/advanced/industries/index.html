Professional Services

At techbrot, we help professional service firms get the most from QuickBooks Online Advanced by configuring systems that reveal performance, streamline access, and simplify client tracking so you can focus on delivering billable work.

  • Role-Based Access Control

    We configure role-based permissions for up to 25 users so each team member sees only what they need, improving security and operational clarity.

  • Client & Project Metrics Tracking

    We set up enhanced custom fields and reports so you can monitor client KPIs and measure engagement, revenue, and retention easily.

  • Project Cost Tracking

    We implement job-level tracking for expenses, income, and labor so you always know true project profitability.

  • Faster Invoicing

    We help you adopt batch invoicing and automation so invoices are created and sent more quickly, reducing administrative overhead.

  • Performance Reporting

    We build dashboards and presentation-ready reports that surface revenue by client, utilization, and margin to support smarter planning.

  • Case & Client Tracking

  • Real-Time Cash Flow

    We configure cash flow dashboards that let you monitor inflows and outflows so you can manage working capital more effectively.

  • Controlled Access

    We set up custom roles and permissions to protect sensitive case files while enabling staff to act on what they need.

  • Expense Automation

  • Board-Ready Reporting

    We produce tailored reports and summaries that keep partners and boards informed with clear, audit-ready data.

  • Cash Flow Visibility

  • Grant & Fund Reporting

    We prepare reporting structures that make pulling financials for grant applications fast and accurate.

  • Role-Based Controls

  • Expense Automation

    We configure dashboards to surface top revenue sources—helping you prioritize bids and services.

  • Job Cost Tracking

    We implement detailed tracking of labor, materials, and subcontractor costs to keep job accounting accurate.

  • Mobile Invoicing

    We enable on-site invoicing workflows so crews can create invoices from the field and speed payment cycles.

  • Project Accounting Insights

    We set up project-based accounting views so you can analyze performance at both job and portfolio levels.
