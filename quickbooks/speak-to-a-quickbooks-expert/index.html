    Process transparency

    What the call actually sounds like.

    No mystery. Every discovery call follows the same four-phase rhythm. 30 minutes total, free, no obligation.

    1. Minutes 0–5

      Context

      Quick orientation. Your business, the QuickBooks version, who’s currently handling the books, what’s working, what’s not.

    2. Minutes 5–20

      Diagnostic

      Specific situation review. We surface what we’re hearing, what’s typical for businesses like yours, and where the actual issue likely lives.

    3. Minutes 20–28

      Recommendation

      What we’d recommend — including “not us” if that’s the honest read. Engagement structure, rough fee range, timeline expectation.

    4. Minutes 28–30

      Next step

      If a fit: written fixed-fee scope within 3 business days. If not a fit: referral toward someone who is. Either way, the conversation ends with a clear next step.
