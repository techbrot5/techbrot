    What TechBrot QuickBooks services are.

    TechBrot QuickBooks services are independent Certified ProAdvisor engagements delivered by a curated network of vetted local accounting practices, serving U.S. small and mid-sized businesses. The QuickBooks silo covers six engagement types — setup & onboarding, file cleanup, migration, consulting & workflow advisory, plan selection, and the ProAdvisor help center — across four QuickBooks products: Online, Desktop, Enterprise, and Payroll. Every engagement is delivered by a Certified ProAdvisor against a written scope, with platform-level quality review. TechBrot is an independent ProAdvisor firm and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Intuit Inc.

    TechBrot QuickBooks services, in five questions.

    What QuickBooks services does TechBrot offer?

    TechBrot offers six independent Certified ProAdvisor service types: setup & onboarding, file cleanup, migration (including Desktop to Online), consulting & workflow advisory, plan selection, and a free ProAdvisor help center. All services are delivered across QuickBooks Online, Desktop, Enterprise, and Payroll. Every engagement is delivered by a named Certified ProAdvisor with platform-level quality review.

    Is TechBrot affiliated with Intuit?

    No. TechBrot is an independent Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor firm. QuickBooks, QuickBooks Online, QuickBooks Desktop, QuickBooks Enterprise, and QuickBooks Payroll are registered trademarks of Intuit Inc. TechBrot is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Intuit. TechBrot operators provide independent expert services that complement — not replace — Intuit’s product support.

    How is TechBrot different from Intuit support?

    Intuit product support resolves software-level issues: installation, billing, account access, basic configuration. Intuit support does not perform bookkeeping work, reconciliation rebuilds, file repair requiring judgment, plan-selection advisory across competitive options, or ongoing operational engagements. TechBrot ProAdvisors deliver that work independently.

    Which QuickBooks products does TechBrot support?

    All of them. TechBrot operators are Certified ProAdvisors across QuickBooks Online (Level 2), QuickBooks Desktop, QuickBooks Enterprise, and QuickBooks Payroll. The same operator network supports every product. Operators also support common integrations including Gusto, Rippling, Shopify, Amazon, Stripe, and bill-pay platforms.

    Who actually does the work?

    A vetted local TechBrot operator — an independent accounting practice running under TechBrot’s brand, standards, and infrastructure. Every operator holds active Certified ProAdvisor credentials, is credentialed, insured, and onboarded against a defined quality standard. Named point of contact, platform-level quality review, continuity guaranteed.

    QuickBooks questions

    What people ask about TechBrot QuickBooks services.

    No. TechBrot is an independent Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor firm. QuickBooks, QuickBooks Online, QuickBooks Desktop, QuickBooks Enterprise, and QuickBooks Payroll are registered trademarks of Intuit Inc. TechBrot is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Intuit. We provide independent ProAdvisor services that complement — not replace — Intuit’s product support.

    • What does “independent ProAdvisor” actually mean?

      It means we’re certified by Intuit on QuickBooks proficiency but operate as an independent firm. Intuit doesn’t pay us, doesn’t direct our advice, and doesn’t share our incentives. We’re free to recommend the right product for your business, including alternatives to QuickBooks when those fit better.

    • Can I call Intuit for help instead?

      Yes, for software-level issues — installation, billing, account access, basic configuration. Intuit support is the right channel for those. For bookkeeping work, reconciliation rebuilds, file repair requiring judgment, or ongoing operational support, an independent ProAdvisor is the appropriate resource.

    • Why are ProAdvisor certifications meaningful?

      ProAdvisor certifications are issued by Intuit after structured training and examination. They verify that an advisor has demonstrated proficiency in a specific QuickBooks product. TechBrot operators hold active certifications across all four products in the QuickBooks stack — meaning they’re qualified to work on whatever you’re running.
