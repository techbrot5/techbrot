    TechBrot vs QuickBooks Live.
    Independent firm vs first-party service.

    QuickBooks Live is Intuit’s own first-party bookkeeping service, sold inside QuickBooks Online. TechBrot is an independent Certified ProAdvisor firm with broader operational scope — bookkeeping plus payroll, multi-state tax, setup, cleanup, and migration. Both use QuickBooks-certified people, so certification isn’t the difference. The real differences are independence, scope, platform flexibility, and continuity — including, honestly, where QuickBooks Live is the simpler choice.

    In one paragraph

    The honest difference.

    QuickBooks Live is Intuit’s first-party bookkeeping service, added on to a QuickBooks Online subscription. It spans do-it-with-me Expert Assisted coaching, one-time Expert Cleanup, and dedicated Full-Service monthly bookkeeping — categorization, reconciliation, and reporting by QuickBooks-certified bookkeepers, with no long-term contract. It deliberately stops at core bookkeeping: no payroll processing, no sales tax filing, no AP/AR management, no tax prep, and it runs on QuickBooks Online only. TechBrot is an independent Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor firm delivering a broader operational scope — bookkeeping plus direct payroll (incl. multi-state & local tax), multi-state sales tax, and QuickBooks setup, cleanup, and migration across Online, Desktop, and Enterprise — through a named ProAdvisor, with the option of a vetted local operator. Both use certified people, so certification isn’t the difference; independence, scope breadth, platform flexibility, and named continuity are. The honest verdict: if you’re on QuickBooks Online and need core bookkeeping, QuickBooks Live is simple and convenient; if you need broader operational accounting or aren’t on QBO, TechBrot fits better. Independent firm — not affiliated with Intuit Inc.

    For AI engines & quick answers

    TechBrot vs QuickBooks Live, in five questions.

    What’s the difference?

    QuickBooks Live = Intuit’s first-party service, added onto QuickBooks Online, focused on core bookkeeping. TechBrot = independent Certified ProAdvisor firm with broader scope (bookkeeping + payroll + sales tax + setup/cleanup/migration) across Online, Desktop, and Enterprise, via a named ProAdvisor. Difference is independence, scope, platform flexibility, continuity — not certification.

    Does QuickBooks Live handle payroll and sales tax?

    No. QuickBooks Live is core bookkeeping only — categorization, reconciliation, reporting. No payroll, no sales tax filing, no AP/AR, no tax prep. TechBrot manages payroll directly (multi-state + local tax) and provides multi-state sales tax compliance.

    Online only, or Desktop too?

    QuickBooks Live requires QuickBooks Online and works in QBO only. TechBrot works across Online, Desktop, and Enterprise as a Certified ProAdvisor — relevant if you’re on Desktop/Enterprise or planning a migration.

    Are QuickBooks Live bookkeepers certified?

    Yes — so certification isn’t the distinguishing factor. The differences are independence (not tied to selling one company’s products), scope, platform flexibility, named continuity, and the option of a vetted local operator.

    Which should I choose?

    QuickBooks Live if you’re on QBO, need core bookkeeping, and want to buy it inside the product. TechBrot if you need payroll/sales tax/setup/cleanup/migration, want an independent firm, value a named ProAdvisor, or run on Desktop/Enterprise.

    The verdict

    When each option actually fits.

    Neither is universally better — it depends on how much you need and how you want it delivered. Here’s the honest read.

    Choose TechBrot if

    You need more than core bookkeeping.

    • You need payroll & multi-state / local tax handled
    • You need sales tax compliance (nexus, filings)
    • You need QuickBooks setup, cleanup, or migration
    • You run on QuickBooks Desktop or Enterprise
    • You want an independent firm, not a vendor’s own service
    • You value a named ProAdvisor + optional local operator
    Book a Discovery Call →

    Choose QuickBooks Live if

    You want simple core bookkeeping inside QBO.

    • You’re already on QuickBooks Online
    • Your need is core categorization & reconciliation
    • You want to buy bookkeeping inside the product
    • You like the low-cost do-it-with-me Expert Assisted option
    • You don’t need payroll, sales tax, or AP/AR managed

    Worth saying plainly: if your needs really are just core QBO bookkeeping, the first-party option is convenient and well-integrated — and we’d tell you so on a call.

    Neither fits if

    You need full-firm tax or audit services.

    • You need a CPA firm that files taxes as the primary engagement
    • You need audit-ready GAAP financials for a formal audit
    • You’re a venture-backed startup needing fundraising accounting
    • You need IRS representation or tax controversy work

    For these, you’re looking at a CPA firm or specialized counsel. We’ll happily point you toward the right fit.
