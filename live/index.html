Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Discover clear answers and expert insights to help you master your bookkeeping and financial management. Empower your business with knowledge that drives success and confidence every step of the way.

What services does techbrot provide?

We offer a full range of QuickBooks services including setup, bookkeeping, payroll, cleanup, and ongoing support—all tailored for small businesses. As certified QuickBooks ProAdvisors, our goal is to keep your finances accurate, tax-ready, and stress-free so you can focus on running your business.

Can you help me set up QuickBooks Online for the first time?

Yes! We manage the entire QuickBooks Online setup process from start to finish:

(i) Build and customize your chart of accounts,

(ii) Connect your bank and credit card feeds,

(iii) Design invoices, reports, and dashboards for your business.

This ensures you start quickly and confidently without missing any important details.

Do you provide ongoing bookkeeping support?

Absolutely. Our monthly bookkeeping packages keep your books accurate and up to date:

(i) Transactions categorized properly,

(ii) Bank and credit card accounts reconciled,

(iii) Key reports like Profit & Loss and Balance Sheet generated.

With ongoing support, you’ll always have a clear picture of your business finances.

Can you clean up old or messy QuickBooks data?

Yes. We specialize in QuickBooks cleanup to get your books back on track:

(i) Fixing misclassified or duplicate transactions,

(ii) Reconciling months or years of accounts,

(iii) Correcting balances so reports accurately reflect your business.

Cleanup ensures smarter business decisions and smooth tax preparation.

Do you handle payroll services?

Yes. We provide QuickBooks payroll setup and ongoing management including:

(i) Employee onboarding and setup,

(ii) Running payroll cycles accurately and on time,

(iii) Preparing and filing payroll tax forms.

This ensures your team is paid correctly while staying compliant.

Do you provide tax filing services?

We don’t directly file taxes. However, we prepare accurate books and detailed reports, and we can coordinate with your CPA or tax preparer to make year-end filing seamless and stress-free.

How do I get started with techbrot?

Getting started is simple. Just contact us for a free consultation. We’ll:

(i) Discuss your business needs,

(ii) Recommend the right service plan,

(iii) Set up QuickBooks and begin ongoing support.

Our goal is to simplify your QuickBooks experience from day one.
