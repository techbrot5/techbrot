What qualifications do the Live Priority experts have?

Our Live Priority experts are seasoned QuickBooks specialists with years of experience. They consistently demonstrate:

(i) Longer tenure compared to standard support teams,

(ii) High issue-resolution skills across diverse QuickBooks topics,

(iii) Outstanding customer satisfaction ratings, ensuring you receive reliable guidance for your QuickBooks questions.

What’s included with QuickBooks Live Priority?

Live Priority gives you access to specialized QuickBooks product experts for customer service, product guidance, and technical support. Here’s what’s included:

(i) Customer service & technical support: Experts are available Monday–Friday, 5 AM–7 PM PT, and Saturday–Sunday, 6 AM–3 PM PT to assist with inquiries and troubleshoot product issues.

(ii) Onboarding & coaching: One-on-one sessions to set up your QuickBooks account, learn features, and receive tailored product guidance. Available Monday–Friday, 6 AM–6 PM PT.

(iii) Hands-on guidance: To ensure you maximize QuickBooks functionality and feel confident with your workflows.

What isn’t included with QuickBooks Live Priority?

Live Priority focuses on product and technical support only. It does not include:

(i) Bookkeeping guidance or tax advice,

(ii) Assistance with accounting decisions or filing taxes.

If you need bookkeeping or tax-related help, consider upgrading to Live Expert Assisted or Live Expert Full-Service Bookkeeping for comprehensive support.

Can I switch plans or cancel anytime?

Yes. QuickBooks Live Priority is fully flexible:

(i) Cancel anytime before your next billing date without penalty,

(ii) Switch to another QuickBooks Live plan directly from the Live Experts dashboard or Settings,

(iii) Adjust your subscription as your business needs change.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. QuickBooks Live Priority operates on a monthly subscription basis with no long-term commitment. You are free to switch plans or cancel at any time after your current billing period ends, ensuring flexibility and peace of mind.
