Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Discover clear answers and expert insights to help you master your bookkeeping and financial management. Empower your business with knowledge that drives success and confidence every step of the way.

Do you offer bookkeeping services?

Yes. We provide online bookkeeping through QuickBooks Live. Depending on the plan you choose, you’ll either get direct guidance from certified bookkeepers or have them fully manage your books for you. Our goal is to make sure your financial records stay accurate, compliant, and tax-ready throughout the year—saving you time and reducing errors.

With virtual support, you can:

(i) Receive answers to your bookkeeping questions quickly,

(ii) Stay compliant and ready for tax season,

(iii) Have confidence that your records are always accurate and up to date.

What qualifications do your bookkeepers have?

Our bookkeepers bring expertise from many industries and all hold active QuickBooks Online ProAdvisor certifications. Each bookkeeper must meet these requirements:

(i) QuickBooks Online ProAdvisor Certification (active),

(ii) QuickBooks Online Advanced ProAdvisor Certification (active),

(iii) At least 3 years of QuickBooks Online experience,

(iv) Plus one or more of the following: CPA license, Certified Public Bookkeeper credential, bachelor’s degree in accounting/finance, or extensive professional experience in bookkeeping and financial management.

How should I prepare before a call with a bookkeeper?

To make the most of your session:

(i) Write down your questions in advance so nothing is missed,

(ii) Choose a time when you can focus without distractions,

(iii) Be signed in to QuickBooks Online on your computer so you can share your screen.

This ensures your bookkeeper can provide clear and personalized guidance right away.

When will my books be cleaned and up to date?

Cleanup is usually completed within 30 days after your bookkeeper receives the necessary documentation. Timelines may vary:

(i) Well-organized accounts are often finished sooner,

(ii) Complex books or delayed documentation may take longer,

(iii) Your bookkeeper will keep you updated and set expectations throughout the process.

Can I change or cancel my plan anytime?

Yes. You have full flexibility with your subscription. You can:

(i) Cancel anytime before your next billing date without penalty,

(ii) Switch to another plan directly from your QuickBooks Online account settings,

(iii) Adjust your subscription as your business grows and evolves.
