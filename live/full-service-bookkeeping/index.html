What’s included in techbrot Full-Service Bookkeeping?

Our Full-Service Bookkeeping includes two phases—cleanup and ongoing bookkeeping—to keep your financial records accurate and tax-ready:

(i) Cleanup & Setup: We organize your chart of accounts, connect your bank accounts, and set up QuickBooks for smooth use. If you already have data in QuickBooks, we’ll clean it up during the first month of service.

(ii) Ongoing Bookkeeping: Each month, your dedicated bookkeeper categorizes transactions, reconciles accounts, and prepares essential financial reports like profit & loss and balance sheet.

(iii) Additional Benefits: You also receive techbrot Priority and Expert Assisted perks, including extended hours, product guidance, and tax resource support.

Note: Full-Service bookkeepers cannot provide specific tax advice or file your taxes for you.

What’s not included in techbrot Full-Service Bookkeeping?

Our service is focused on accurate and up-to-date bookkeeping. However, it does not include:

(i) Sending invoices or paying bills,

(ii) Managing inventory, accounts receivable, or accounts payable,

(iii) Financial advisory or investment services,

(iv) Providing or filing tax returns,

(v) Creating or sending 1099s,

(vi) Payroll management (QBO Full-Service Payroll is available separately).

I already use QuickBooks Online. Can I still sign up for techbrot services?

Yes. You can subscribe to techbrot’s bookkeeping services whether you’re already using QuickBooks Online or just getting started. The process is simple:

(i) Sign in to your QuickBooks Online account,

(ii) Choose the techbrot service plan that fits your needs,

(iii) Contact our team for setup assistance and pricing details if needed.

This flexibility ensures you can start receiving expert support without switching platforms.

How much time will I get with my dedicated bookkeeper each month?

There’s no strict time cap. Many small businesses typically schedule one or two video check-ins per month to review results and ask questions. Here’s what you can expect:

(i) You’re limited to one scheduled session at a time,

(ii) You can share documents with your bookkeeping team anytime,

(iii) Chat and communication support are available outside of meetings.

This balance allows for both structured check-ins and flexible on-demand support.

Can I switch plans or cancel whenever I want?

Yes. With techbrot, you maintain full control of your subscription. You can:

(i) Cancel at any time before your next billing date without penalty,

(ii) Switch between plans as your business needs change,

(iii) Adjust your subscription so your service grows with your company.
