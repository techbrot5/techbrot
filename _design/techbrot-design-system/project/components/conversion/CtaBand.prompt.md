CTA band — the dark conversion block that closes every page (formalized `.section--final-cta`). One per page, at the end.

```jsx
<CtaBand
  eyebrow="Speak to an expert"
  heading="Bring us the file. We'll tell you what it needs."
  lede="A 30-minute diagnostic call with a Certified ProAdvisor — not a salesperson."
  primary="Book the diagnostic"
  secondary="See pricing"
  disclaimer="TechBrot is an independent accounting firm and Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor. We are not Intuit. QuickBooks and Intuit are trademarks of Intuit Inc."
/>
```

The `disclaimer` is mandatory on every QuickBooks-related page.
