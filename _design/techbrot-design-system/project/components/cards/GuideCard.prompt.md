Editorial guide/article card — oxblood uppercase category, serif title, excerpt. Use in 3-up `featured-guides__grid` wrappers for content/resources sections.

```jsx
<div className="featured-guides__grid">
  <GuideCard
    category="QuickBooks"
    heading="QuickBooks Online vs Desktop in 2026"
    excerpt="What the Desktop sunset actually means for your file, and when migration is urgent."
  />
</div>
```
