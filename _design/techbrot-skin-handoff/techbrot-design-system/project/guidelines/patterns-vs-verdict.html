The verdict

For most US small businesses with payroll, QuickBooks Online. Past $25M or multi-location inventory, neither — and we'll say so.

Choose QuickBooks Online if…

  • You run US payroll and want it native
  • Your accountant network matters — ProAdvisors are everywhere
  • You lean on the app ecosystem (ecommerce, POS)

Choose Xero if… (honest column)

  • Price matters and payroll doesn't — Xero stays cheaper
  • You need unlimited users without per-seat fees
  • Multi-entity bookkeeping on a budget
