```jsx
<Callout tone="info" title="From the research">
  Teams that reconcile weekly close 4.2× faster than teams that reconcile at month-end.
</Callout>
```

Tones: `note` (default, neutral) · `info` · `positive` · `warning` · `critical`. Full hairline border by design — never a colored left border.
