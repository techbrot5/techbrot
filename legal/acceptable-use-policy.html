  • Harassment, threats, or hate speech toward employees, clients, or third parties.
  • Interfering with other users’ access or functionality of the Services.

3.5 Spam, Phishing, or Fraudulent Activity

  • Using Services to commit scams, financial fraud, or identity theft.
