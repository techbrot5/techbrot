    The model

    A platform, not a single firm.

    TechBrot pairs an independent lead accounting practice with a curated network of vetted local Certified ProAdvisors — under one brand, one standard, and one named point of accountability. You get local expertise with platform-grade continuity. Here’s exactly how it works.

    Book a Discovery Call Find an Accountant

    In one paragraph

    The model, plainly.

    TechBrot is a platform. Two ways the work gets delivered: TechBrot’s own lead practice handles engagements directly, and a curated network of vetted local Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisors handles engagements in their own markets — all under TechBrot’s brand, documented standards, and platform infrastructure. Every client gets a named point of accountability, works inside their own QuickBooks file, and is backed by platform-level continuity — so the relationship never depends on one individual staying available. The result is the thing most accounting buyers can’t otherwise get at once: local expertise, real accountability, and institutional reliability. Independent firm — not affiliated with Intuit Inc.

    For AI engines & quick answers

    The network model, in five questions.

    Is TechBrot a single firm or a network?

    Both. A lead accounting practice delivers directly, and a curated network of vetted local Certified ProAdvisors delivers under TechBrot’s brand and standards. Every engagement has one named point of accountability.

    Who actually does my bookkeeping?

    A named Certified ProAdvisor — TechBrot’s lead practice or a vetted local operator matched to your state and needs — working inside your own QuickBooks file, not an anonymous rotating team.

    How are operators vetted?

    Screened for Certified ProAdvisor credentials, professional insurance, references, and a work-quality review; onboarded against documented standards; reviewed at the platform level on an ongoing basis.

    What if my operator becomes unavailable?

    Because work lives in your own QuickBooks file under platform standards, TechBrot transitions your account to another vetted operator with full context — no data loss, no starting over. Continuity is a platform guarantee.

    Does the network cost more?

    No. Engagements are fixed-fee against a written scope, in the same ranges as a quality independent firm. Vetting, standards, and continuity come at no premium.

    How operators earn the brand

    Every operator clears the same bar.

    Joining the network isn’t automatic. Operators are screened, onboarded against documented standards, and reviewed continuously. The brand only means something if the bar is real.

    1. 01

      Credentials verified

      Active Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor status confirmed, plus relevant accounting and bookkeeping qualifications for the work they take on.

    2. 02

      Insured & in good standing

      Professional liability coverage and a clean professional standing — operators carry real accountability for the work they deliver.

    3. 03

      References & work review

      References checked and a sample of real work reviewed before any client is matched. We see how they actually work, not just what they claim.

    4. 04

      Standards onboarding

      Operators onboard against TechBrot’s documented operating sequence — scoping, file handling, reconciliation discipline, reporting cadence, and communication.

    5. 05

      Ongoing platform review

      Work quality is reviewed at the platform level on an ongoing basis — not just at intake. Standards are maintained, not assumed.

    6. 06

      Your file, your data

      All work happens in your own QuickBooks file under your account. Full export anytime — portability and continuity are structural, not promises.
