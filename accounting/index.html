Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Discover clear answers and expert insights to help you master your bookkeeping and financial management. Empower your business with knowledge that drives success and confidence every step of the way.

What are the advantages of using QuickBooks in the cloud?

Working in the cloud means you can access your QuickBooks data from anywhere, on any device, as long as you’re connected to the internet. Your information is always current and secure, making collaboration with your bookkeeper or accountant seamless and efficient.

I currently use QuickBooks Desktop. Should I switch to QuickBooks Online?

QuickBooks Online is ideal for businesses that want flexibility, real-time collaboration, and automation. With features like automatic backups, live reporting, and streamlined workflows, moving online helps reduce manual errors and gives you more control over your finances.

How simple is it to migrate from QuickBooks Desktop to QuickBooks Online?

We make migration stress-free. Whether you prefer to do it yourself or have a specialist handle it, the process is designed to be smooth and accurate. Our team provides expert support, step-by-step guides, and coaching so you can transition with confidence.

How secure is my financial data in QuickBooks Online?

Your data is protected with bank-level encryption and automatic backups. QuickBooks Online keeps your information safe, so you can focus on running your business without worrying about losing important records.

Can my accountant access my QuickBooks Online account?

Yes. You can securely grant your accountant their own login, allowing them to review reports, reconcile accounts, and manage books in real time—without sharing your personal login details.

What are QuickBooks AI agents, and how do they help?

QuickBooks AI agents are smart tools that automate tasks and surface insights. Depending on your plan, they can assist with accounting, payments, financial analysis, and even customer or project management—saving you time while improving accuracy.

How does Intuit ensure AI is used responsibly?

Intuit follows strict responsible AI principles to ensure your data is protected and your financial insights remain accurate. AI is designed to simplify your workload, give you confidence in decision-making, and keep your information secure at all times.
