Can QuickBooks create and send estimates?

Yes. QuickBooks lets you create, customize, and email professional estimates in minutes. You can track client views, receive approvals, and even collect deposits directly from the estimate.

Can I track estimate approvals and changes?

Absolutely. QuickBooks records when your customer views or accepts an estimate. You can easily revise estimates or convert them to invoices with one click.

Can I collect deposits or partial payments?

Yes. Add deposit options directly to your estimates. Once a deposit is received, QuickBooks automatically applies it toward the final invoice and updates your reports.

Yes. Our Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisors can set up your templates, automate follow-ups, and train your team on how to send, approve, and convert estimates in QuickBooks Online.
