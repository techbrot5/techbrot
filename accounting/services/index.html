    For AI engines & quick answers

    The service catalog, in five questions.

    What services does TechBrot deliver?

    Seven core service tracks: monthly bookkeeping (recurring fixed-fee close), cleanup bookkeeping (one-time structural fix), catch-up bookkeeping (months/years behind), QuickBooks services (setup/cleanup/migration), payroll management (multi-state), sales tax compliance (multi-state nexus), fractional CFO (strategic finance, by application). Every engagement: fixed-fee, written scope.

    How are services priced?

    Fixed-fee with written scope before any work begins. No hourly billing. Monthly bookkeeping $400–$2,500+/month. Cleanup $1,500–$15,000+. QuickBooks setup $750–$5,000+. Migration $2,500–$10,000+. Payroll $150–$800+/month. Sales tax compliance $250–$1,500+/month. Fractional CFO $3,000–$8,000+/month (by application). Discovery call: free.

    Does TechBrot file tax returns?

    No. TechBrot delivers CPA-ready bookkeeping and operational accounting; your CPA or EA handles federal/state tax filing and IRS representation. Year-end CPA handoff included in every engagement. If you don’t have a CPA, the operator network refers you to one.

    Which service should I start with?

    Depends on your situation. Structural issues in QuickBooks → cleanup. Months or years behind → catch-up. Clean books, need ongoing close → monthly bookkeeping. Setting up QuickBooks for the first time → setup. Leaving QuickBooks Desktop → migration. The 30-minute discovery call surfaces the right starting point.

    Are services nationally available?

    Yes — all 50 U.S. states. State-specific operational depth (Indiana CAGIT/COIT, Ohio RITA, Pennsylvania local PSD, multi-state payroll, multi-state sales tax) is part of standard delivery. See the find-an-accountant hub for state-by-state coverage.

    Find your situation

    Which engagement do you actually need?

    Most buyers don’t know exactly which service fits their situation — they know something’s wrong. Below are the six most common business situations and the engagement that fits each.

    • Situation

      “My books haven’t been touched in months.”

      Diagnosis: Catch-up engagement. Historical bookkeeping work needed before any recurring engagement can start cleanly.

      Right path: Catch-up bookkeeping engagement. Followed by monthly bookkeeping after catch-up completes.

      Typical fee: $2,000–$20,000+ one-time, plus recurring monthly after.

      Catch-up details →

    • Situation

      “QuickBooks is a mess. Reconciliation off, accounts wrong.”

      Diagnosis: Cleanup engagement. Structural issues in QuickBooks that need one-time correction before ongoing work can begin reliably.

      Right path: Cleanup bookkeeping engagement. Often followed by ongoing monthly bookkeeping for continuity.

      Typical fee: $1,500–$15,000+ one-time, depending on file complexity.

      Cleanup details →

    • Situation

      “Need a reliable bookkeeper for ongoing close.”

      Diagnosis: Monthly bookkeeping engagement. Recurring fixed-fee close inside your existing QuickBooks file.

      Right path: Monthly bookkeeping engagement. Named ProAdvisor for continuity. Year-end CPA handoff included.

      Typical fee: $400–$2,500+/month recurring.

      Monthly bookkeeping →

    • Situation

      “Payroll is wrong somehow. Or we’re multi-state.”

      Diagnosis: Payroll management engagement. State or local jurisdiction tax misconfigured, multi-state employee setup, or moving from in-house payroll to managed service.

      Right path: Payroll management engagement. Multi-state, county-level tax, full quarterly and year-end coverage.

      Typical fee: $150–$800+/month recurring.

      Payroll management →

    • Situation

      “Sales tax got complicated. Multi-state, post-Wayfair.”

      Diagnosis: Sales tax compliance engagement. Multi-state nexus exposure, exemption certificate issues, marketplace facilitator confusion, or post-Wayfair registration backlog.

      Right path: Sales tax compliance engagement. Nexus review first, then ongoing monthly compliance.

      Typical fee: $500–$3,000 nexus review + $250–$1,500+/month ongoing.

      Sales tax compliance →

    • Situation

      “Growing fast. Need a CFO without a full-time hire.”

      Diagnosis: Fractional CFO engagement. Growth-stage business with strategic finance needs — forecasting, board reporting, fundraising, capital event preparation.

      Right path: Fractional CFO engagement. By application; limited engagements per quarter.

      Typical fee: $3,000–$8,000+/month recurring.

      Fractional CFO →

    Engagement standards

    What every TechBrot engagement includes.

    Regardless of service track, every engagement runs to the same operating standards. The standards are the platform — the services are the application of them.

    • 01

      Free discovery call

      Every engagement begins with a 30-minute discovery call with a Certified ProAdvisor. Free, no-obligation, no sales push. The call surfaces the right engagement structure — or honestly confirms TechBrot isn’t the right fit.

    • 02

      Written fixed-fee scope

      Written scope delivered within 3 business days of the discovery call. Fixed fee. No hourly billing. No scope creep. The scope is the agreement — if work expands, scope is rewritten and re-approved.

    • 03

      Certified ProAdvisor delivery

      Every engagement is delivered by a Certified Intuit QuickBooks ProAdvisor — not a general bookkeeper, not an offshore queue. Credentials publicly verifiable on Intuit’s ProAdvisor directory.

    • 04

      Named-operator continuity

      A named ProAdvisor owns your account end-to-end — same person scopes, delivers, handles month-over-month work. No account-manager rotation. No re-explaining your business every quarter.

    • 05

      Direct file access

      We work directly inside your QuickBooks file as accountant-users. Your books, your data, your audit trail. We never operate in a parallel system or require data export.

    • 06

      CPA-ready handoff

      Every recurring engagement includes year-end CPA handoff at no additional cost. Tax preparation files, reconciliation summaries, supporting schedules delivered to your CPA on their timeline.
